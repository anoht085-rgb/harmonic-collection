Perspective

Mattern shows how the same sound can feel different depending on the context: a comforting car door “clunk,” a manipulative slot-machine jingle, a calm laptop startup tone. Here, the same 1980s photographs shift with the screen.

On mobile they read as an emotional scrapbook of the decade — one image at a time, with diary-style captions.

On desktop they become a research grid, annotated with themes and references, like an art director’s toolkit for building an ’80s campaign.
